Admissions

Akontombra Senior High School welcomes applications from qualified students seeking admission. We strive to make the admission process transparent and straightforward.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or proof of age
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Official BECE results from previous school

How to Apply

Interested students can apply by visiting the school office or downloading the application form from our website. For more information, please contact our admissions office.
