History

The school was established and absorbed by the Ghana Education Service in January, 1994 through the initiative of the chiefs and people of Akontombra in the then Sefwi Wiawso District who unanimously realized the dynamics of secondary school education on the socio-economic development of individuals and the society at large.

The Akontombra Senior High School is basically a Day School. Yet it has hostel facilities that can accommodate about 150 students. There is a Six-unit classroom block with office and store. As at the time of this documentation

Mission

  • To provide a holistic education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, and social development of our students.
  • To foster a culture of excellence, integrity, and respect within the school community.
  • To prepare students for higher education and responsible citizenship.
  • To promote lifelong learning and personal growth.

vision

  • To be a leading institution in academic excellence and character development.
  • To empower students to become critical thinkers, effective communicators, and responsible citizens.
  • To create a safe and inclusive learning environment that celebrates diversity.
  • To foster partnerships with parents, alumni, and the community for the betterment of our students.
